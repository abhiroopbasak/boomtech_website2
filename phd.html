Appeal for Recruiters




Dear Recruiters,
On behalf of Rajendranath College of Polytechnic, Durgapur, West Bengal, I would like to introduce my institution as an organization of total pledge to quality and excellence in academic quests. It is my proud privilege to present a short overview on RCP.
RCP is one of the venerated organizations in engineering educational institution in the Eastern region of India.Since the year of 2008, we are fabricating technically sound Diploma Engineers in the field of MECHANICAL CIVIL, ELECTRICAL Engineering and COMPUTER SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY.
We initiate ourselves as the trailblazer to showcase a flourishing approach towards higher education for students. We are crafting major training & placement opportunities for the student community. Our Training &Placement Department caters to the need of mentoring & grooming of students to furnish them with all employ ability parameters i.e. Aptitude & Logical skills, Behavioral skills, Technical skill, Communication skill etc to make them an asset to whichever organization they join. Training & Placement Department are also into arranging internship in various Govt. / Private Concerns.
I would like to extend my cordial invitation to visit our Institute for Campus Recruitment purpose of RCP for our passing out batch of Diploma Engineering students and also to establish a long term relationship with our institute. It will be our proud privilege to expand all needed facilities and I assure our recruiters that they will get technically sound, disciplined and properly trained fresher who will convey with paramount honesty & hard work.
I earnestly desire that this will be a great & fertile relationship in the making.

Director, Training & Placement

Functionalities



CareerDevelopment & Guidance cell of Training & Placement Department providesan all-round profession solution to the students. We guide the students to maketheir mind up and chase their dream careers in different fields in Engineering & Technology.
We take care of:
Career Development & Guidance Program.
Industrial / Vocational Training.
Industry & Job Relevant Training.
Communication, Networking & Relationship Building with Potential Recruiters.
Pre Placement Training: Logical Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, English Communication are taught by experts. Mock Interviews & Group Discussions are taken regularly to ensure the students success in the long run.
Career Development Training Program:
Training program via audio-visual pedagogy.
Logical Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, English Communication are taught by experts faculties.
Mock Interviews & Group Discussions are taken in regular interval to ensure the students accomplishment in the long run.
All the training is been provided at free of cost!
Industrial /Vocational Training:
We provide with Short term Industrial / Vocational Training with the maximum number of students of 5th / 6th Semester in Government / Public / Private sector Industries /In-house. This training helps the students to get familiar with the Industry.
FREE JELET tutorials:
We conduct JELET coaching classes for the students who are willing to go for higher studies.
Experts from different disciplines have been preserved to conduct the classes. They constantly increase the skill to obtain them high ranks in JELET Examinations.
The Coaching is free of cost to the students!
Back Office Operations:
Prepare record of the students who undergo internships and the companies which come for recruitment and undergo via On-Line Database Management System.
