Rajendranath College of Polytechnic is a venture of Rajendranath Education & Welfare Trust. The need for establishing this college was felt keeping in mind the growing demand of qualified technical personnel for theIndian industries. Being in the business of steel making for so many years now we have found that there is real shortage of educated technical staff. It hasbeen seen that though there is no dearth of skill and those having skill are earning good wages, but as soon as we talk of their technical knowledge it isreally disappointing. Had those people been technically educated along withtheir acquired skill they could have become much more effective and would havecontributed in a much better way to their respective organizations. As a whole the industry would have been benefited. In our country the need for technically qualified people is huge.Knowing that everybody cannot pursue engineering studies, diploma engineering course is an option for them. By pursuing Diploma engineering courses, one canbe ready for the job at the minimum possible time. But here comes the issue ofselection of genuine good college. In the present day it is quite a difficulttask since so many institutions have come up in the recent days.
One has to be very judicious while choosing the institution. Here atRajendranath College of Polytechnic, we emphasis for disciplined and valueadded education for our students. We shall continuously strive for developmentof the college curriculum keeping an eye to the present day requirement of thestudents and that of the industries. We believe in total development of thestudents in making them ready for the industries. We sincerely believe thatstudents passing out of our college will be our ambassadors in the coming days.

Anti Ragging Policy

principal@rpcollege.ind.in

Ragging in any form is a criminal offence and strictly prohibited inside and outside the Institute. As per the directives of Hon. Supreme Court, All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and Govt. of West Bengal, stringent actions are recommended against students for pandering to and encouraging ragging.
Whosoever unswervingly or indirectly commends, participates in, abets or proliferates ragging within or outside the educational institution shall on certainty, be punished as per the rules. If a student is drawn in any act of ragging prior to taking admission then such student will be denied admission in the Institute.
However, if at a later stage it is found that an admitted student has indulged in any act of ragging then such student will be rusticated.

Disciplinary Committee

principal@rpcollege.ind.in

Discipline committee is a bridge between students and authorities. The most important intention of the Disciplinary committee is to deal with and efficiently unravel various student related matters. The committee has to uphold poise between student’s demands/ problems and viability of explanation provided by authorities.
ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
· To ensure peaceful and nonviolent academic atmosphere in the campus.
· To steer clear of physical confrontation among students.
· To carry out enquiries on report of in disciplinary activities among students
· To commence model actions against students involved in indiscipilinary activities
· To initiate steps to diminish hostility, altercation in future
STUDENTS HAVE A RESPONSIBILITY:
· To respect the possessions and safety of others.
· To contribute to a co-operative learning environment.
· To attend class prepared to learn.
· To maintain the rules and regulations of the college given in the information brochure, provided to the students during admission.
PARENTS HAVE A RESPONSIBILITY:
· To support College policy in a positive way.
· To assist in their child’s learning as a partner with the College in matters concerning homework, uniform, attendance, parent-teacher interactions and behavior.

We make sure zero tolerance of harassment, of any kind, of girls and women on campus.
