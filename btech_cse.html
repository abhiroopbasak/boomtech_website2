Bachelor of Technology - Computer Science and Engineering



In our Computer Science & Technology department, we have planned to create globally competent manpower for the information and communication technology (ICT) industry, teach the recent industrial standards of Hardware, Software & Networking operation, scheduled maintenance, preventive maintenance etc. Our objectives are accomplished by including an optimal mix of fundamental theory subjects and practical, current and industry relevant subjects in the scheme of study. The students will build specialized knowledge in the field of Computer Sciences & Technology with the flexibility to follow their interest through the choice of varied optional modules, courses and electives. We are committed to provide our students the efficient learning to update themselves regularly about market trends & practices, frequent changing trend of modern computer technology.
A few of the salient features of our Institution are the witnesses of our commitment:
  • State of the art infrastructures including modern Computer Lab with fully LAN combined environment; Original & latest versions of Operating & Application software, well equipped

  • Microprocessor, Digital & other Laboratories. High precision & proper calibrated tools & instruments to develop practical hand on skill of our students through workshop & lab activities.

  • Guidance of a dedicated team of qualified and eminent faculties & Lab professionals who have sufficient industrial exposure to guide our students to be familiar with industrial know-how.

  • Excellent Library facilities attached with spacious reading room & equipped with LAN combined computers along with wide range of study materials in the form of books, magazines, specialized Journals & E-Books in proud association with British Council.

  • A dedicated training & placement cell to provide “on the job”vocational industrial training during the tenure of the course and 100% placement assistance to our students at various sectors of Information Technology, Hardware & Software industries, Steel, Power, Consultancy, Automobile, Aviation, Indian railway, Defense etc.

  • Intake capacity: 60 students per year
