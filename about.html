About us

Rajendranath College of Polytechnic (RCP) was founded in the year of 2008 and aimsto serve the exclusive purpose of delivering quality education in engineering fieldin the state of West Bengal, India. Presently, it constitutes with fourbranches i.e. Mechanical, Electrical, Civil Engineering and Computer Science& Technology. It offers a three years (six semesters) Diploma programme inEngineering leading to the award of Diploma in Engineering.

The Institute is situated at Durgapur, which is the fastest emerging city of West Bengal, India andit has grown up to become one of the mega city of eastern India. It ispopularly known as the Steel city of eastern India and varying its image in thefield of education to become one of the multipurpose cities of India.

RCP addresses the need for trained and specialized technicalprofessionals for Industry worldwide. It aims to inflate the opportunities forstudents to develop intellectual knowledge based with leadership skills tocompete in the global arena. This purpose is being addressed through a numberof technical education programs.

RCP is all about trustworthiness. It has its own ability or purpose toenvisage, build and convey an Institute of high academic standard. RajendranathCollege of Polytechnic aims to create a better India by efficiently preparingthe youth to cater to the technological advancements of the century. Theinnovative teaching techniques have set the students apart from the rest, thusmaking RCP one of a kind.

RCP has a visionof conveying truly world‐class, quality education. With access to thecurriculum delivered by a highly qualified and committed faculty, as well asthe right pedagogy and environment, RCP students gain an education with afuture perspective.


Mission

Rajendranath College of Polytechnic has the mission to create andsustain an academic environment conducive to academic and professionalexcellence. To create environment to foster education with relevant research anddevelopment. To provide world class technical education commensurating thepresent academic requirements. To create avenues of technical education andresearch in new and emerging academic fields. To create atechnology savvy campus and to impart value based education. To develop facultycompetence, to meet the challenges of rapidly changing socialenvironment. To nurture innovative andcreative abilities of students and faculties. To be afinishing school where the outgoing students will be ready to accept the challenges of theeducational society.

‘Rajendranath Education and Welfare Trust’ has established ‘RajendranathCollege of Polytechnic’ with the above stated mission and vision under thechairmanship of Shri Jayanta Chakrabarty.
