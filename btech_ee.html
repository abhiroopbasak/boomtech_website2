Bachelor of Technology - Electrical Engineering



Designing, Developing, Testing and Monitoring ofelectrical equipment is what Electrical Engineering is allabout. In Electrical Engineering department in RCP, we preparestudents in order to make them suitable for power generation plants, powersupply & distribution organization, Steel, Automobile etc, includingvarious operations & maintenance areas, project area, planning area etc.All electrical standards as per IS/BS/ISO will come under our enhanced syllabus.
A few of the salient features of our Institution are the witnesses of our commitment:
  • State-of-the-art infrastructures including modern electrical workshop, laboratories.
  • Guidance of a dedicated team of qualified and eminent faculties & Lab professionals who have sufficient industrial exposure to guide our students to be familiar with industrial know-how.
  • Excellent Library facilities attached with spacious reading room & equipped with LAN combined computers along with wide range of study materials in the form of books, magazines, specialized Journals & E-Books in proud association with British Council.
  • A dedicated training & placement cell to provide “on the job” vocational industrial training during the tenure of the course and 100% placement assistance to our students at reputed companies in various sectors like Steel, Power, Consultancy, Automobile, Aviation, Indian railway, Defense etc.
  • Intake capacity: 60 students per year
